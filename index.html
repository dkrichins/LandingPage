About Me

Dustin Richins

Since 2008, I have been the owner of Synergistic Web Solutions, a software development LLC located in Boise, ID. I have spent most of the time the last few years working for two primary clients: The Blue Cross Foundation of Idaho and RTR Engineering and Resource Management. While at the Blue Cross Foundation, I have worked on several preventative and risk management IT projects associated with tobacco use, childhood obesity, and childhood physical fitness and nutrition. For RTR Resource Management I worked primarily on project management work, federal and state environmental permit authoring, and geotechnical data storage. I am married and have a super awesome 11-year old daughter, and during my free time I like getting outdoors and having fun!!
